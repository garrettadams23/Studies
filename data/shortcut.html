Military Code
NATO Phonetic Alphabet · Military Time · Rank Codes
NATO/ICAO phonetic alphabet eliminates letter confusion over voice/radio. Used by military, aviation, emergency services, and amateur radio (ITU). Example: FUBAR → Foxtrot · Uniform · Bravo · Alpha · Romeo. Military time runs 0000–2359; spoken as "oh-six-hundred" for 0600.
